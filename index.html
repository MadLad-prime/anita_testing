Old Roots, New Ground

Woke Coffee was born from a paradox: a deep love for the ancient rituals of coffee, and a frustration with an industry slow to innovate. We asked, "What if we could honor the past while pioneering the future?"

We pair heritage beans from storied farms with 21st-century roasting science to give you a transparent journey from the farm to your cup. Every sip is an awakening.
